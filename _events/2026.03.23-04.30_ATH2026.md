Advanced Training and Professional Development Course on “Accessible Textual Heritage.”

A 25-hour program combining lectures and hands-on laboratory sessions covering:
* Accessible Computing
* Accessible Computational Resources
* Accessible Digital Documents
* Accessible Research Outputs

The course is taught in English and offered in a blended format (online and in person).

Registration by February 8, 2026. [More information](/assets/pdf/CPAP-ATH2026.pdf).
