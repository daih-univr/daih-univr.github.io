[Gimena del Rio Riande](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8997-5415
) (Universidad de Buenos Aires / CONICET)

Participants will be introduced to the concepts of minimal computing and accessibility in the context of Digital Humanities studies.
The first part of the seminar will focus on minimal computing tools, which aim to foster equity and develop inclusive practices in the field of digital humanities.
The second part will include a hands-on session with the [CollectionBuilder tool](https://collectionbuilder.github.io/), aimed at creating a digital collection of cultural objects. Special attention will be paid to standard metadata and open science best practices.
